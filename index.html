Reversion Alpha DCA Pro

Buy more when it matters.
Hold back when it doesn't.

The risk score model automatically concentrates your buying power at the moments of greatest opportunity — the deep dips — while conserving capital when valuations are stretched.

⚡ The Risk Score Model
A 500-day geometric moving average measures how cheap or expensive any asset is relative to its own long-term trend — from 0.0 (extreme buy) to 1.0 (extreme caution).
🎯 Precision DCA
Linear or exponential multipliers automatically scale your buy amount based on the risk score. A $1,000 base becomes $3,000 in a moderate dip and $5,000 at the extreme.
📊 Advanced Strategies
Simulate sell strategies that trim at market tops, LEAP options at the deepest dips for leveraged exposure, and covered calls that generate income when your holdings are overvalued.
💼 Portfolio Tracker
Monitor all your real holdings simultaneously. Live prices, gains, risk scores, and Buy/Hold/Reduce signals across every position — including a planned target allocation tool.

The answer wasn't gut feel or financial media noise. It was a systematic, data-driven framework — one that measures where an asset is relative to its own history, and acts accordingly.

These two platforms are the result of that work. They don't predict the future. They remove emotion from the equation and replace it with discipline, process, and precision.
